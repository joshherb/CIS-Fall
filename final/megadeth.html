Megadeth is an American Heavy Metal band formed in Los Angeles in 1983. The band was formed by Dave Mustaine and David Ellefson following Mustaine's dismissal from Metallica. They are known as one of the big four bands of Thrash metal alongside Metallica, Anthrax and Slayer.
