WELCOME TO METAL WIKI,

Here you will find all info on songs, albums, and artists complete with lyrics and videos. Heavy metal is a genre of rock music that developed in the late 1960s and early 1970s. With roots in blues-rock and psychedelic rock, the bands that created heavy metal developed a thick, heavy, guitar-and-drums-centered sound, characterized by highly amplified distortion and fast guitar solos. Metal Wiki is the place for all things Heavy Metal -- the artists, the bands, the music and more!
